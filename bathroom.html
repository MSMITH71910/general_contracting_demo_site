Bathroom Remodeling Services

Every bathroom project is managed by our licensed team — plumbing, tile, electrical, and finishes all under one roof.

Walk-In Showers

Custom tile showers, frameless glass enclosures, rain heads, body sprays, and steam systems. We do full pan installation and waterproofing.

Soaking & Freestanding Tubs

Freestanding soaking tubs, whirlpool tubs, and alcove replacements. Plumbing rough-in and freestanding filler installations included.

Custom Tile Work

Floor-to-ceiling tile, mosaic accents, subway tile, large format porcelain, and natural stone. Expert layout and precision installation.

Heated Floors

Electric radiant floor heating under any tile surface. Smart thermostats with programmable schedules — walk into warmth every morning.

Custom Vanities

Single and double vanities, floating vanity installations, vessel sinks, undermounts, and custom mirror/medicine cabinet combinations.

Ventilation & Electrical

Exhaust fan upgrades, GFCI outlet installation, vanity lighting, recessed lighting, and smart vent fan with humidity sensor.
