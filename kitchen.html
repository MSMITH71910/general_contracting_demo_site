Kitchen Remodeling

Dream Kitchen Renovations

From cabinet refreshes to full gut renovations — Apex delivers kitchens that combine beauty, function, and lasting quality. Starting at $25,000.

What's Included

Full Kitchen Remodeling Services

One team handles every trade — no subcontractor handoffs, no surprises.

Custom Cabinetry

Semi-custom and full custom cabinets in shaker, flat-panel, raised panel, and inset door styles. Dovetail drawers, soft-close hardware, and painted or stained finishes.

Countertops

Quartz, granite, marble, quartzite, and butcher block. Full fabrication and install including sink cutouts, edge profiles, and backsplash integration.

Plumbing & Fixtures

Sink upgrades, garbage disposal, pot filler, dishwasher, and refrigerator water line. Full rough-in for layout changes.

Electrical

Under-cabinet lighting, pendant lighting over islands, outlet upgrades, dedicated appliance circuits, and smart switch integration.

Tile & Backsplash

Ceramic, porcelain, glass, and natural stone backsplash installation. Full height tile behind ranges and hood vents.

Layout Redesigns

Wall removal, island additions, pantry built-ins, and full kitchen reconfiguration. We handle structural engineering and permits.
